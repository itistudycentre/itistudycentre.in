📖 How to Download

🪪 NCVT Admit Card

  1. Click on NCVT Admit Card.
  2. Open the official NCVT MIS website.
  3. Select Trainee Profile.
  4. Login using your PRN Number and Date of Birth.
  5. Download your Admit Card.

📄 ITI Marksheet Download

  1. Login to the NCVT MIS Portal.
  2. Open Trainee Profile.
  3. Select Marksheet.
  4. Download your ITI Marksheet PDF.

🏆 ITI Certificate Download

  1. Login to the NCVT MIS Portal.
  2. Open Trainee Profile.
  3. Select Certificate.
  4. Download your Digital Certificate.

📢 Important Information

  • ✔️ Use only the Official NCVT / SCVT Website.
  • ✔️ Keep your PRN Number and Date of Birth ready.
  • ✔️ Marksheet and Certificate are available after successful declaration of result.
  • ✔️ If the website is slow, try again after some time.
  • ✔️ Never share your login credentials with anyone.
